Hand Controls

Guide to Features and Use

Scroll

  • To begin, bring hand up and make a sideways pinch. When the arrows meet the scroll bar, you are ready to start scrolling.
  • To scroll slowly, pinch and make a small movement with your hand.
  • To scroll quickly, pinch and throw in desired direction. Be sure to fully open hand when you have completed the throw.

Media Controls

  • To begin, fire up your favorite media player.
  • Point at the screen. Be sure to tuck your thumb in tight to the palm.
  • To PLAY/PAUSE, point up and through the top menu piece.
  • To go to PREVIOUS TRACK, move pointer through the menu piece on the left.
  • To go to NEXT TRACK, move pointer through the menu piece on the right.
  • To set VOLUME, move cursor down to slider and move left or right.
  • To exit, open hand fully or put your hand down to your side.

Application Switcher

  • To activate the Application Switcher, bring your open hand up parallel to the floor. Flip your hand upside down. Push up and through the target on the top of the screen.
  • To switch to an open application, grab it from the carousel and drop it in the center of the screen.
  • To exit, drop your hand down to your side.
